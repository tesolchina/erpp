Welcome to the Teaching and Learning Research Team

Dr. Mable Chan

Chair, Teaching and Learning Research Team

Welcome to the Teaching and Learning Research Team. As Chair of this dynamic team, I am delighted to collaborate with each of you in advancing research that deepens our understanding of language acquisition and improves our educational methods.

We are committed to equipping students with essential skills in academic, professional, intercultural, and digital communication, preparing them for both intellectual growth and global participation. Our wide-ranging programs are thoughtfully aligned with the institution's mission to ensure students excel in their studies and future careers.

Our Core Focus Areas:

  • Exploring and Securing Support: We aim to identify and secure funding and incentives to boost scholarship activities, ensuring well-supported and impactful research.
  • Vetting Funding Applications: Our team carefully reviews and endorses funding applications, ensuring alignment with our strategic goals.
  • Engaging with Experts: We collaborate with experts and consultants to offer seminars, workshops, and consultations, aiding professional development.
  • Fostering Research Projects: We encourage research projects related to teaching and learning in the belief that inquiry and investigation are essential to educational excellence.
  • Promoting Collaboration: We aim to coordinate and encourage teamwork among colleagues on both current and new research initiatives.
  • Data Governance and Analytics: We are committed to enhancing data governance and analytics capabilities, promoting data-driven decision-making.

Moving forward, our Research Team is committed to providing unwavering support for our colleagues. Our goal extends beyond our department, reaching across different disciplines to promote interdisciplinary collaboration. This approach will enrich the experiences of both undergraduate and postgraduate students.

I am excited about the opportunity to work closely with each of you. Together, we will explore the realms of pedagogical research and address emerging technologies that are shaping our future. Let us innovate, inspire, and make a meaningful impact in the field of teaching and learning.
